# GASTROTATOR: USER REFERENCE MANUAL
Version 1.0 (March 2026)

Welcome to GastRotator. This manual provides information on how to utilize the Kinetic Recipe Extraction system and manage your local culinary database.

## [HOME] INTRODUCTION & HOME SCREEN
The Home Screen is your "Kitchen." It displays all recipes stored locally on your device.
* **The List:** Scroll through your recipes. Each card shows the dish name, category, and calories (if available).
* **Magic Button:** Tap the (+) button to enter the Import Screen.
* **Safety:** Long-press a recipe if you wish to remove it from your database.

## [IMPORT] IMPORTING RECIPES (AI Magic Import)
GastRotator uses advanced AI to "read" cooking videos.
* **YouTube URL:** Copy a URL from the YouTube app and paste it here.
* **Magic Wand Icon:** Tap this to begin the extraction. The app will fetch the transcript and use Gemini AI to organize it.
* **Placeholder Mode:** If you don't have an API key yet, the app creates a "Placeholder" so you can save the video link for later.

## [DETAIL] VIEWING & COOKING
The Detail Screen is optimized for active cooking.
* **Ingredients:** Listed at the top. Note that volume measurements are automatically converted to grams for accuracy.
* **Instructions:** Numbered steps.
* **Nutritional Data:** Located at the bottom. Includes Total Calories and kcal/100g.

## [SETTINGS] SETTINGS & API CONFIGURATION
This app is decentralized. You are responsible for your own AI connection.
* **Gemini API Key:** You must get a free key from "Google AI Studio." Paste it here to enable recipe extraction.
* **Visual Theme:** Choose a color preset or use the "Kinetic Orange" default.
* **Storage:** You can view how many recipes are taking up space on your device.

## [FAQ] FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
**Q: Does this app store my data online?**
A: No. Every recipe, transcript, and API key stays on your phone.

**Q: Why did my import fail?**
A: Ensure the video has captions available. If it's a very new video, YouTube might still be generating the transcript.

**Q: Is the calorie count accurate?**
A: It is an AI estimation based on the ingredients list. Always use professional equipment for medical-grade nutrition tracking.
